# Make It Funny

## A DJ project, a tattoo, and a rule for life

**Whatever you do, make it fun.** It is not a campaign line; I have *Make It Funny* tattooed on my stomach.

For me, fun is not noise or a shortcut around quality. It means bringing energy without ego, making hard things approachable, and creating moments people remember.

That same instinct shows up in product work: details matter, personality matters, and the experience around the result matters.
